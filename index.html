My Vacation

Day 1: Arrival

I arrived at my hotel in the afternoon and checked in. The room was spacious and had a great view of the ocean.

Day 2: Beach Day

I spent the whole day at the beach, soaking up the sun and swimming in the ocean. The water was warm and clear.

Beach Volleyball

In the afternoon, I played beach volleyball with some other tourists. It was a lot of fun, but also quite tiring!

Day 3: Exploring

On the third day, I rented a bike and went exploring. I visited some local markets and tried some delicious food.

Local Market

The market was crowded and noisy, but it was worth it for the amazing food. I tried some fresh fruit, grilled meat, and seafood.

Seafood

The seafood was especially good. I had some grilled fish with lemon and herbs, and it was so tasty.

Day 4: Relaxation

On the last day of my vacation, I just relaxed by the pool and read a book. It was a nice way to end the trip.

Goodbye

After four days of sun, sand, and relaxation, it was time to say goodbye to the beach and head back home.
